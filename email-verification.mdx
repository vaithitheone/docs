## Why verification matters

Verifying your email secures your account and ensures you receive alerts, invoices, and important product updates.

## How it works

<Steps>
<Step title="Sign up or sign in">
Create an account or sign in with your email address.
</Step>

<Step title="Check your inbox">
We send a verification email with a secure link. The link expires for your safety.

</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="I didn't receive the email">
- Resend from the banner in the app
- Confirm your email address is correct in Settings
- Whitelist `no-reply@gellerwash.com`
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="The link expired">
- Request a new verification email from the app
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
